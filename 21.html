We left on June 29 at about noon. We didn't have a set plan, so up highway 287 we went. Jay and I were both excited to leave. Our eyes and minds were opened during the first 10 miles. Untill a spare tire fell off a trailer in front of us. The tire rolled into the other lane and hit a new ford truck. As we passed through the accident scene the tire flew passed on the right side at about 70 miles an hour. The realities of how vulnerable motorbikes are was setting in.
