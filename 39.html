Still groggy, we board a sea plane and fly across the Cook Inlet. We are dropped onto a mass of floating vegetation. We boat to a red salmon spawning spot. Bears swim right up along the boat and dive after fish. Incredible! Lunch at the Greek palace, great pizza. We arrive in Seward to find out that every hotel/motel is full. We found a dormitory room at the holiday inn. Lobby was "stuffed" with dead animals. Cold showers are awesome.
