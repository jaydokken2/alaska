Luke's birthday, I swear we didn't forget about it, we were just in the middle of nowhere! More rain all day. Jay thinks he saw a billboard for a brewery. I ask a local at the gas station for directions to downtown "this is down town" he replies. We are both delirious from being in the elements and could not stop laughing. People thought we were the crazy ones. We are both thinking constantly about hot tubs and warm climates. Didn't think it could get any worse. Jay crashes his bike on a rain slicked muddy road on our way to a mosquito infested campsite with very little, wet wood! The glassed Dease Lake and the little cloud break made it all worthwhile. I catch on to the ancient art of the "hip ditch".
