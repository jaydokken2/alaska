Moose sighting. As we crossed the Yellow head River there was an expresso stop $4, and country store where we bought fishing line, two cans of tuna and some crackers, $18. Fire wood for our Tangle lakes campsite along the Denali highway, $7. Jay catching his first 4" fish, priceless. We thought we would be reeling in 40 lb fish with our homemade poles. We were wrong. About twenty miles into the tundra we passed a man who ran out of gas. He pulled a gas pump out of the back of his truck and filled up.
