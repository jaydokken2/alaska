The bluest water I've ever seen. We hiked up on a receding glacier. Very much what I think a moonwalk would be like. Next gas 90 miles, oops. Almost made it. Jasper subway. Friendly Canadians everywhere. Rain suits on for the first time, not thinking that I would live in mine the next two weeks. If you're ever in Prince George, never, I mean NEVER stay at the Hi-way Motel. Nothing was changed since the early sixties and it reeked of urine. I didn't even remove my clothes when I got in bed. We should have kicked the owner's ass for renting us that unit. I now ask to see the room before I rent. Our nature count was up this day; 4 bears, 2 moose, 1 elk, and 1 exploding tire. Up until now, I never experienced an 18-wheeler tire explosion but I always see the rubber on the side of the road.
